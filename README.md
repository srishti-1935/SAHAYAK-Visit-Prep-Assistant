# 🌿 Sahayak — Visit Prep Assistant

> Know before you go.

Sahayak helps patients navigate Indian government hospitals by generating a personalised prep card in their language — before they leave home.

## What it does

Enter your symptoms, hospital, age, language, and scheme card. Sahayak instantly tells you:
- Which OPD department to go to
- Exactly which documents to bring
- What to say at the billing counter to claim your scheme
- What questions to ask your doctor
